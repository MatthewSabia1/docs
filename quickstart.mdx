## Get started with AdRecon

This guide walks you through signing in, exploring the ad feed, saving ads, creating projects, and capturing landing pages.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to AdRecon">
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Magic Link">
        1. Go to [app.adrecon.io](https://app.adrecon.io)
        2. Enter your email address
        3. Click **Send Magic Link**
        4. Check your inbox and click the link
        5. You'll be redirected to the `/app` dashboard

        <Note>
          Magic links expire after 60 minutes and can only be used once. If you don't see the email, check your spam folder.
        </Note>
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore the ad feed">
    The dashboard displays **100,000+ tracked ad creatives** from four affiliate networks.

    **Three viewing scopes:**
    - **All Ads** — Browse the full database
    - **Saved Ads** — View your bookmarked collection
    - **Project** — Filter by project folder

    **Two display modes:**
    - **Table view** — Compact rows for quick scanning
    - **Grid view** — Large cards for visual browsing

    Each ad shows:
    - Creative image or video
    - Offer name and advertiser
    - Niche and network classification
    - Days running and status
    - Landing page URL
    - Ad copy text
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save your first ad">
    Two ways to save:
    - **Quick save** — Click the bookmark icon on any ad row or card
    - **From modal** — Open the ad detail modal and click **Save Ad**

    <Info>
      An undo toast appears for 5 seconds after saving or unsaving. You can find all saved ads under the **Saved** tab.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a project">
    1. Click the **Projects** tab
    2. Enter a project name in the sidebar input
    3. Click the **+** button or press Enter
    4. Your project appears in the sidebar

    **Assign ads to projects:**
    - Open a saved ad's modal
    - Click **Manage Save**
    - Check the project boxes
    - Click **Save**

    <Info>
      Project names are case-insensitive and must be unique across your account.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Capture a landing page">
    1. Click any ad to open the detail modal
    2. Find the **Page Ripper** section (non-Meta URLs only)
    3. Click **Rip Landing Page**
    4. Wait for capture to complete (up to 2 minutes)

    The ZIP archive contains:
    - `page.html` — Self-contained HTML snapshot
    - `assets/` — CSS, JS, images, fonts, and media files

    <Warning>
      Page Ripper cannot capture Meta platform URLs (Facebook, Instagram, Threads). Only public landing pages are supported. Rate limit: 10 captures per 15 minutes.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common workflows

**Research a niche:**
1. Set Niche filter to your target vertical
2. Sort by Days Running (descending) to find proven winners
3. Save the best ads to a project

**Build a swipe file:**
1. Browse All Ads with different filter combinations
2. Quick-save interesting creatives
3. Organize into projects by campaign or client

**Analyze a competitor:**
1. Search by advertiser name or offer
2. Review their creative variations
3. Rip their landing pages for funnel analysis
