## What is AdRecon?

AdRecon is a SaaS ad intelligence platform that gives you direct access to a curated database of **100,000+ active ad creatives** across the largest affiliate networks in the world:

- **ClickBank**
- **Digistore24**
- **BuyGoods**
- **MaxWeb**

Whether you're a media buyer, affiliate marketer, or creative strategist — AdRecon helps you find proven winners faster.

## Key Capabilities

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Live Ad Feed" icon="database">
    Browse a continuously updated feed of ad creatives with images, landing page URLs, network classification, and taxonomy tagging. View in table or grid mode with real-time filtering.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Save & Organize Ads" icon="bookmark">
    One-click saving with project-based organization. Build swipe files, organize by campaign or niche, and access your collection from any device. Row-level security keeps your data private.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Landing Page Ripper" icon="download">
    Capture self-contained HTML snapshots of ad landing pages using headless Chromium + SingleFile. Download complete ZIP archives with all assets for offline analysis. Rate limited to 10 captures per 15 minutes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Multi-Network Coverage" icon="network-wired">
    Unified schema across ClickBank, Digistore24, BuyGoods, and MaxWeb. Normalized metadata with media prioritization and automatic niche classification.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Data Quality

All ad data flows through the `ads_feed_v2` normalized view, ensuring consistent metadata across networks. Active monitoring keeps the database fresh with performance indicators like days running and status tracking.
